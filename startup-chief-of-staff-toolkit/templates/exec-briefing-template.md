# Executive Briefing Template

**Purpose:**  
Give the CEO / leadership team everything they need to make a decision in 5–10 minutes, without having to read a 20-page deck.

---

## 1. One-line summary

> In one sentence: what is this about and what do you want?

---

## 2. Decision needed

- **Type of decision:** (e.g. Go / No-Go, Budget approval, Prioritisation)
- **Deadline for decision:**
- **Decision owner:** (e.g. CEO, Founder, Leadership Team)

---

## 3. Context (max. 5 bullets)

- Why this topic matters now  
- What has changed (trigger)  
- What is at risk if we do nothing  
- Relevant constraints (time, budget, regulation)

---

## 4. Options

**Option A – Recommended**

- Short description  
- Pros  
- Cons  
- Impact (qualitative + if possible quantitative)

**Option B**

- Short description  
- Pros  
- Cons  
- Impact

---

## 5. Recommendation

**My recommendation:**  
> Clear, direct recommendation.

**Rationale (3–5 bullets):**

- …
- …
- …

---

## 6. Execution outline (if decision is "Yes")

- Owner(s)
- Key milestones
- Immediate next steps (next 1–2 weeks)
- Key risks & mitigations
